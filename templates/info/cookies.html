What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Google Chrome Mozilla Firefox Apple Safari Microsoft Edge
Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data
  • Android: Chrome → Settings → Site Settings → Cookies
  • Third-Party Apps: Check individual app settings
Note that blocking cookies may affect your experience on our website.
