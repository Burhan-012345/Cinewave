Account & Profile

Questions about accounts, profiles, and settings

Creating a CineWave account is easy:

  1. Click "Register" at the top right of the page
  2. Enter your name, email, and create a password
  3. Check your email for the verification OTP (valid for 10 minutes)
  4. Enter the OTP on the registration page
  5. Your account is now ready to use!

You can also sign up using Google for a faster registration process.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the Forgot Password page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 10 minutes)
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Login with your new password
Password requirements: 8+ characters, uppercase letter, number, special character, no spaces

Yes! CineWave supports multiple profiles:

  • Each account can have up to 5 profiles
  • Each profile has its own watch history and recommendations
  • You can create kids profiles with content restrictions
  • Switch between profiles easily from the profile menu

To create a new profile:

  1. Go to your Profiles page
  2. Click "Create Profile"
  3. Enter a name and choose settings
  4. Click "Create"

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Account Settings
  2. Scroll to "Account Management"
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your password
  5. Select a reason for deletion
  6. Click "Delete My Account"
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data, including watch history and preferences, will be permanently deleted.

Billing & Payments

Questions about subscriptions, payments, and refunds

We accept various payment methods:

Credit/Debit Cards
  • Visa
  • MasterCard
  • American Express
  • Discover
Digital Wallets
  • PayPal
  • Google Pay
  • Apple Pay
  • Samsung Pay

All payments are processed securely with encryption and fraud protection.

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Go to Account Settings → Subscription
  2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  3. Select your reason for cancellation
  4. Confirm the cancellation
Note: Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again after cancellation.

Our refund policy:

  • Monthly subscriptions: No refunds for partial months
  • Annual subscriptions: Pro-rated refunds within 30 days
  • Technical issues: Case-by-case evaluation

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team with your account details
  2. Explain the reason for the refund request
  3. Provide any relevant information or screenshots

To update your payment information:

  1. Go to Account Settings → Billing
  2. Click "Payment Methods"
  3. Click "Add Payment Method" or "Edit" on existing methods
  4. Enter your new payment details
  5. Click "Save"
Make sure to update your payment method before your next billing date to avoid service interruption.

Questions about watching movies and TV shows

For best performance, use a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi when possible.

Yes! Offline viewing is available with a Premium subscription:

  • Download movies and shows to your device
  • Watch without an internet connection
  • Available on mobile apps and desktop
  • Downloads expire after 30 days
  • Up to 100 downloads at a time

To download content:

  1. Find the movie or show you want to download
  2. Click the download button (downward arrow)
  3. Choose the video quality
  4. Wait for the download to complete
  5. Find your downloads in the "Downloads" section

The Continue Watching feature:

  • Automatically saves your progress in movies and shows
  • Works across all your devices and profiles
  • Saves your exact timestamp when you pause or stop
  • Appears on your dashboard and Continue Watching page
  • Shows progress percentage for each item

To resume watching:

  1. Go to your Continue Watching page
  2. Click on any movie or show
  3. It will resume from where you left off

Content availability varies by region due to:

  • Licensing agreements: Content providers grant rights by territory
  • Distribution rights: Exclusive rights in certain regions
  • Local regulations: Content restrictions in some countries
  • Release schedules: Different premiere dates worldwide

What you can do:

Technical Support

Questions about technical issues and troubleshooting

If you're experiencing buffering or poor video quality:

  1. Check your internet speed: Run a speed test at speedtest.net
  2. Restart your router: Unplug for 30 seconds, then replug
  3. Use a wired connection: Ethernet is more stable than Wi-Fi
  4. Reduce quality: Try a lower resolution in video settings
  5. Close other apps: Bandwidth-intensive apps can interfere
  6. Clear cache: Clear your browser or app cache
  7. Update software: Ensure your browser or app is updated

If the app won't load or keeps crashing:

  1. Update the app: Check for updates in your app store
  2. Restart your device: Power cycle your phone, tablet, or TV
  3. Clear app cache: Go to device settings → Apps → CineWave → Clear Cache
  4. Reinstall the app: Uninstall and download again
  5. Check storage space: Ensure you have enough free storage
  6. Update device OS: Make sure your operating system is updated
If problems persist, contact support with your device model and OS version.

Common error codes and solutions:

Error Code Meaning Solution
1001 Connection lost Check internet connection
2003 Video unavailable Try another title
3005 Account issue Log out and back in
4002 Region restricted Content not available in your area
5001 Server error Try again in a few minutes

To report a technical problem:

  1. Go to the Contact Us page
  2. Select "Technical Support" as the subject
  3. Include as much detail as possible:
    • What you were trying to do
    • What happened (error messages, screenshots)
    • Device and browser information
    • Steps to reproduce the issue
  4. Submit the form

Our technical team will investigate and get back to you as soon as possible.
